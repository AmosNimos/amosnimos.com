Internet Relics - Remains of the Old Web

A beautiful homage to the era of the free and decentralized internet. It captures the essence of individually created, handcrafted HTML sites that reflect the creativity and uniqueness of their creators. This project not only preserves the history of the internet but also reminds us of the importance of maintaining a decentralized and open web.

This collection features screenshots and links to sites, automatically selected at random from http://wiby.me/surprise/ using a custom script I developed.
